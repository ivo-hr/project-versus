stepX: 	walking/running sounds. to be played one after another.

shield: 	shield activated sound

taunt: 	taunt sound

jumpX: 	jumping sounds. "0" plays if jumping from the ground, rest are midair jumps in order

atkX: 	attacking sounds. to be played randomly between themselves

specN: 	special move sounds. They go as follows:
	U: 		up-special
	D: 		down-special
	S: 		left/right-special
	N: 		neutral-special
